Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

1. Overview

Quiet Anchor is designed with privacy in mind. This privacy policy explains how the app handles user data.

2. Data Collection

Quiet Anchor does not collect, sell, or share personal data with third parties.

3. Local Data

Information entered in the app may be stored locally on the user’s device. This may include entries, reflections, preferences, or other app-related content created by the user.

4. iCloud

If iCloud sync is enabled by the user, app data may be synced through Apple’s iCloud service. In that case, Apple’s privacy policy and the user’s iCloud settings apply.

5. Analytics and Tracking

Quiet Anchor does not use third-party advertising SDKs or third-party tracking.
